--- wordpress_id: 171 title: Group Blackmails Microsoft for Xbox Freedom wordpress_url: http://spaetzel.com/PermaLink,guid,267.aspx layout: post --- Group Blackmails Microsoft for Xbox Freedom
In pretty crazy news, a Xbox "research group" says that they have figured out a way to run Linux on the Xbox without any modchip or other hacks. They have blackmailed Microsoft, if MSFT doesn't release an official Xbox version of Linux, they'll release their pirate version--essentially eliminating all copyright protection on the Xbox.
It will be intersting to see what happens here.
