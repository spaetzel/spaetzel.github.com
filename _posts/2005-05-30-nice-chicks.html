--- wordpress_id: 373 title: Nice Chicks wordpress_url: http://spaetzel.com/PermaLink,guid,98.aspx layout: post --- I'd been meaning to take a photo of the ducks that swim in the creek right by my building for a while now, just was always in a hurry to be somewhere. Today I saw a nice duck on the bank so I got out my camera. Just as I got it out I see this mother run into the water with her whole family. I was so lucky to see so many newborn chicks.
Sadly the pics came out quite out of focus, I'm not sure why. Maybe I need to bring my tripod with me next time...
