They start off with a picture of my first bus of the trip. Turns out that I managed to miss my 8:45 bus by showing up at 8:30, so I ended up taking this one which was an Express to Toronto. So instead of going though Guelph and Cambridge I went straight to Toronto, so it was a good turn of events.
But showing up early to Toronto just meant that I had even more time to kill before I could catch my 12:30Am bus to Timmins, 2 and a half to be exact. So I killed time by wandering downtown Toronto. See if you can name all the places that I saw.
Not that many, but enough for a bunch of nice pictures.
