You all know the feeling - Seeing something that you really thought would have made a great photo, only to not have your camera with you.

Well today, as with every Thursday my backpack was quite full and I didn't bring my camera with me. When I was coming back from band practice I see a small rabbit standing on the sidewalk ahead of me very peacefully.
I magaged to get pretty close to him before he ran off.

It was a very pretty scene, lots of snow all around, rabbit sniffing away.

Hopefully my description will be good enough, I'll sure try to always have my camera with me from now on!
