--- wordpress_id: 118 title: Jade Empire = Awesome, Butterfly Effect = Really Good wordpress_url: http://spaetzel.com/PermaLink,guid,517.aspx layout: post --- Today I completed Jade Empire. What a great game. I fully enjoyed every moment playing that game. What a great story. Some moments moved along pretty slow. Some fights were a little annoying. But all together it was quite wonderful. I am missing it right now, I wish that the game kept on going.

To finish off the night, I loaded up The Butterfly Effect. This was an excellent movie. I love movies that make you think and this one sure did. I loved the mystery of what will happen next. So many things can change so quickly in this movie. Ashton Kutcher was actually quite good in it, and I wasn't stuck thinking of his character in That's 70s Show at all. I recomend it highly to anyone who doesn't mind a weird movie, and wants to think a little.
