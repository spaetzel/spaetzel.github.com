--- wordpress_id: 116 title: Canadian DMCA Introduced wordpress_url: http://spaetzel.com/PermaLink,guid,519.aspx layout: post --- Well it looks like Canadians have now caught up to the draconian US Copyright restrictions. Bill C-60 has been made law and it adds pretty much the same restrictions to Canadians and the DMCA did to Americans. Now it is illegal to break any protection schenme placed on digital media even if it something as simple as ROT-13. You can read the full text of the bill here, or read this excellent blog entry on it.
