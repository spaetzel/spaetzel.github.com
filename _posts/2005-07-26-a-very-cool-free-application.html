--- wordpress_id: 112 title: A very cool free application wordpress_url: http://spaetzel.com/PermaLink,guid,524.aspx layout: post --- I don't know if any of you have every heard of Konfabulator. It is a very cool app that lets you put cool little widgets up on your desktop.

I currently am using it to show a clock, photos from my flickr contacts, my battery life, the westher in Waterloo and my current CPU usage. There are all sorts of other widgets that you can download for it as well.

Well, the great people at Yahoo went up and bought the company that makes Konfabulator, and now the program that used to cost $30 is now free. When I heard the news I pounced on it. For the nerdly ones of you out there, I highly recommend that you check it out!
