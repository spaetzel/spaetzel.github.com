The last few days I have been packing away getting ready to move to London on Saturday. I've barely had enough time to stop and relax. The only time that I'm not doing something productive is when I'm having my evening chat with Laura-Lee. I sure enjoy that and look forward to our talk tonight.



Last night I headed to Mount Hope Cemetery in Kitchener for my second photoroam with a bunch of local Flickr users. It was enjoyable and I got a lot of photos. As Opal Posted the mood was quite different this time from the last. The people weren't quite as crazy.



Hopefully, I'll be able to edit and post my photos soon. But I've been so busy with packing that I haven't had nearly enough time. And this thursday, I'll be headed to downtown Waterloo with the Photoroamers again to document the Waterloo Busker festival.



At work today I managed to finish the script that I'd been working on for the last few days. I am quite proud of how well it works especially considering how little time it took me. It allows you to upload a Maple worksheet to MaplePrimes and it spits back an HTML version of the worksheet. So you can then post the HTML anywhere in MaplePrimes. It is pretty cool, check it out.
