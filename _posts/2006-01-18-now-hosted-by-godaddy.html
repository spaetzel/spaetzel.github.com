--- wordpress_id: 69 title: Now Hosted by GoDaddy wordpress_url: http://spaetzel.com/PermaLink,guid,570.aspx layout: post --- Well, I was getting fed up with the unreliablility of redune.com. The server seemed to be taking down my DSL connection regularly, and it looked like the server couldn't handle the amount of traffic that was going through it.

I was looking at renewing a domain name that was about to expire when I saw that GoDaddy.com offers web hosting at less than $5.00 a month. This seemed like too good of an offer to pass up.

So I signed up and tested the service out. It looks quite good. With the basic, economy package I get 5GB of storage, and 500GB of transfer per month! And I am able to use MySQL and PHP pages as much as I want.

This should mean that Redune will be much more reliable in the future, which definitly is a plus for both myself and my readers.

If you have any issues with the server, please let me know.
