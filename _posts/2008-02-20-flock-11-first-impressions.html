In the past, every time that I install Flock, I find that the browser is horribly slow, making it unrealistic to use as my everyday browser. But this time, it looks like they got it right. It doesn't seem to be any slower than Firefox. Now, I think the only complaint I have is that the People bar listing for Twitter is pretty much useless because it doesn't update often enough, and people's tweets are cut off, so I have to go to Twitter.com to see the whole post.
